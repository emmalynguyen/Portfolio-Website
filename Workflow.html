Life During COVID — Zine Cover

The National Art Honor Society created a Zine to showcase students' perspectives during the pandemic. I designed the front cover and my own personal spread using Adobe Illustrator.

I focused on the graphics on colorful gradients and soft edges. The cover page has dainty but elegant, star-like designs to represent finding the small instances of hope and beauty in the midst of a cautious time.

The Atlas Organization — Mobile App Design

The Atlas Organization aims to uplift voices by displaying artworks, sharing interviews from different community members, and spreading knowledge of history and culture.

While their website is in motion, I wanted to take it a step further and design a mobile interface using their website's current interface.

Robotics Merch Graphic

In high school, I was on the robotics team. Our team required a lot of merch to be designed, and I designed our team tshirt. The theme for the competition was space.

I used Illustrator, Star Wars font, our team logo, and galatic-themed structuers to create the final design.

Red Riding Hood
