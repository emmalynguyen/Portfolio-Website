I'm a visual and interaction designer from Southern California who is currently based in Atlanta, Georgia. As a second-year student studying computational media with concentrations in people and interaction design, I thrive working in a field that combines art and computer science. Some of my favorite go-to tools include Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, Lightroom, and XD. In my free time, I like to journal, collect stickers, obssess over fonts, and (attempt to) cook with my friends! As a designer, I want to focus on inclusive and accessible design. I want to help create more sustainable products and take advantage of the constantly-evolving technology. I want to create a world of better design.
